Our Shared Memoir

Dear Soul friend,

We probably don’t know each other, but we are more akin than you might think. Even as strangers, we carry echoes of each other’s stories. Our paths could be completely different — but haven’t we gone through the same phases of life? Haven’t we been dealt similar cards of pain, guilt, and regret?

At our core, don’t we all crave happiness, compassion, and peace? Isn’t the desire to be loved, understood, and respected something we universally share — whether by ourselves or someone else?

We all get lost while trying to find clarity, doubt ourselves, and dread the worst possibilities. At times, we get hopeless too.

But that’s what makes us empathize with each other. That’s why we don’t always need a reason to be kind. Maybe we’ve not walked in each other’s shoes — but we’ve kind of tried them on. So why not give the benefit of the doubt? Why skip to the ending when we don’t even know the whole story?

Let’s pause before we draw conclusions. Let’s read between the lines before we draw the line.

Let’s share a fragment of time that we’d both highlight in our own memoirs.
